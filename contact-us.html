📋 Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Do you offer scholarship application assistance?
We provide informational articles and guides, but we do not offer personalized application assistance or consulting services.

❓ Can you help me find a job with visa sponsorship?
Our job articles provide information about LMIA-approved positions and visa sponsorship opportunities. However, you must apply directly through the employer or official job portals.

❓ Is Ziphynet really free?
Yes! All our content is completely free. We never charge users for scholarship or job information.

❓ How can I report a scam?
If you encounter anyone claiming to represent Ziphynet asking for payment, please report it to legal@ziphynet.com immediately.

❓ Can I submit a guest post or collaborate?
Yes! Please email partnerships@ziphynet.com with your proposal.
