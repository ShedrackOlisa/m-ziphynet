US Visa Pathways for Tech Professionals

H-1B Visa (Specialty Occupations)

The H-1B program allows US employers to temporarily employ foreign workers in specialty occupations requiring a bachelor's degree or higher .

2026 H-1B updates :

  • Annual cap: 65,000 regular + 20,000 master's exemption
  • FY 2027 cap has been reached as of April 2026
  • New weighted selection process effective Feb 27, 2026 favors higher-wage workers
  • Computer occupations receive ~65% of H-1B visas

EB-2 Green Card (Advanced Degree / Exceptional Ability)

For professionals with master's degrees or bachelor's + 5 years experience .

Current priority date: ~5 years for Chinese-born applicants

NIW option: 2+ top-tier conference papers, ~150 citations (reduced from 200 for 2026)

EB-3 Green Card (Professional / Skilled Worker)

For professionals with bachelor's degrees or skilled workers with 2+ years experience .

Current priority date: ~4 years for professionals category

O-1 Visa (Extraordinary Ability)

For individuals with extraordinary ability in sciences, education, business, or athletics. No annual cap. Wordware offers O-1 sponsorship for exceptional candidates .

L-1 Visa (Intracompany Transfer)

For managers, executives, or employees with specialized knowledge transferring from an overseas office to a US location.

⚠️ EB-2/EB-3 Timing Warning: The April 2026 Visa Bulletin saw significant advancement in EB-3 priority dates, but retrogression is possible. Time is of the essence – consult an immigration attorney .

Step-by-Step Application Process

Step 1: Build In-Demand Skills

Focus on AI/ML, cloud architecture, MLOps, data engineering, or cybersecurity. Consider certifications: AWS Solutions Architect, Azure AI Engineer, Google Cloud Professional Data Engineer .

Step 2: Target Visa-Sponsoring Employers

Research companies with H-1B sponsorship history using the USCIS public database. Prioritize :

  • FAANG companies (Google, Meta, Amazon, Apple, Netflix)
  • Y Combinator startups (often more flexible with visas)
  • Data center infrastructure companies

Step 3: Optimize Your Resume for AI Screening

2026 hiring processes use AI for initial resume screening. Include specific technologies and measurable achievements .

Step 4: Secure OPT (For Graduates)

STEM graduates get 36 months of OPT – three chances for H-1B lottery. Use the first year for skill development, second year for H-1B application, third year for green card sponsorship initiation .

📋 Required Documents for Tech Visa Applications:
  • Valid passport
  • Degree certificates and transcripts (with English translations)
  • Educational credential evaluation (ECA) for foreign degrees
  • Employment verification letters (2+ years experience for EB-3)
  • Technical portfolio / GitHub profile
  • Professional certifications (AWS, Azure, etc.)
  • Published research or patents (for EB-2 NIW)
  • Recommendation letters from supervisors/professors
